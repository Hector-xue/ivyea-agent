# Amazon Canada restricted-products diagnostics

Source basis: authenticated Canada Seller Central restricted-products policy and the live selling-application workflow.

## Required evidence

- CA marketplace, ASIN/SKU, product type/category, brand, condition, intended use, claims, and fulfilment route;
- exact restriction or approval message and the governing Canadian help-page section;
- requested licence, test report, declaration, invoice, label, or safety documentation;
- selling-application status and case history.

## Guardrails

- Category approval, product legality, and FBA eligibility are separate decisions.
- Do not use a US approval as proof of Canadian eligibility; Canadian rules (including bilingual labelling and Health Canada / CFIA requirements) can differ.
- Never advise changing category, brand, claims, or ASIN to conceal the product.
