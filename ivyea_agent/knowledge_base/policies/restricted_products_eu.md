# Amazon EU restricted-products diagnostics

Source basis: authenticated European Seller Central restricted-products policies and store-specific selling applications.

## Required evidence

- exact EU store, ASIN/SKU, product type, brand, claims/use, manufacturer/responsible-person data, and fulfilment route;
- live restriction text, policy reference, and requested documents;
- applicable test reports, declarations, labels, registrations, traceability, and safety evidence;
- whether the restriction is Amazon-wide, store-specific, category-specific, or FBA-specific.

## Guardrails

- Do not treat one EU store's approval as automatic approval everywhere.
- Amazon approval does not replace GPSR, CE, EPR, chemical, VAT, or customs obligations.
- Never recommend hiding regulated characteristics or relisting to bypass review.
