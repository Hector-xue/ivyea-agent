# Amazon Japan restricted-products diagnostics

Source basis: authenticated Japan Seller Central restricted-products policies and live selling applications.

## Required evidence

- JP marketplace, ASIN/SKU, product type/category, brand, condition, claims/use, importer/manufacturer, and fulfilment route;
- exact Japanese restriction/approval message and current policy reference;
- requested licence, test, label, safety, invoice, or regulatory evidence;
- application and case history.

## Guardrails

- Do not reuse US/EU approvals or compliance conclusions for Japan.
- Distinguish Amazon category approval, Japanese legal requirements, and FBA eligibility.
- Preserve the original Japanese notice and avoid widening a translation.
