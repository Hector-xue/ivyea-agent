# Amazon Canada Account Health evidence workflow

Source basis: the authenticated Canada Seller Central Account Health dashboard and performance notifications.

## Required evidence

- exact CA marketplace/account, dashboard state, policy category, and affected ASIN/order;
- complete performance notification, stated root cause, requested action, and deadline;
- order, invoice, compliance, supplier, or corrective-action evidence relevant to that notice;
- submission history, case replies, and current status.

## Decision boundary

- The live dashboard and notification control the current requirement; generic metrics or old forum posts do not.
- Separate order-performance, policy-compliance, product-safety, IP, and verification issues.
- A state change does not identify the cause unless the associated notification does.

## Guardrails

- Do not reuse a US appeal template without matching the Canadian notice.
- Never promise reinstatement or invent a threshold.
- Do not submit fabricated invoices, plans, or compliance evidence.
- Store only redacted, authorized exports.
