# Amazon UK restricted-products diagnostics

Source basis: authenticated UK Seller Central restricted-products policy and the live selling-application workflow.

## Required evidence

- UK marketplace, ASIN/SKU, product type/category, brand, condition, intended use, claims, and fulfilment route;
- exact restriction or approval message and the governing UK help-page section;
- requested licence, test report, declaration, invoice, label, or safety documentation;
- selling-application status and case history.

## Guardrails

- Category approval, product legality, and FBA eligibility are separate decisions.
- Do not use a US approval as proof of UK eligibility.
- Never advise changing category, brand, claims, or ASIN to conceal the product.
