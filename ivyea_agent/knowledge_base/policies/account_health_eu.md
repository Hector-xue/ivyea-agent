# Amazon EU Account Health evidence workflow

Source basis: the authenticated European Seller Central Account Health dashboard and store-specific notifications.

## Required evidence

- exact EU store and account, dashboard status, notice language, policy category, and affected item/order;
- full notification, requested remedy, deadline, prior submissions, and case replies;
- store-specific legal/product-compliance evidence and supply-chain records;
- whether the action is local to one store or affects linked European stores.

## Decision boundary

- Linked EU access does not mean one policy notice or remedy applies identically to every store.
- Use the current store's dashboard and notification; translate without widening its meaning.
- Separate Amazon policy evidence from VAT, EPR, CE/GPSR, customs, and other legal advice.

## Guardrails

- Do not copy a US appeal template without matching the EU notice.
- Never recommend evading enforcement by relisting or changing truthful product identity.
