## What the official source establishes

- Manage Your Experiments is an Amazon tool for testing versions of eligible listing content such as titles, images, bullet points, descriptions, or A+ modules depending on eligibility.
- The tool helps sellers compare customer response to content variants over a defined experiment period.
- Eligibility and supported experiment types depend on account, brand, ASIN, marketplace, and current Amazon program rules.
- Experiment results should be interpreted with enough traffic and stable offer conditions.

## Operational interpretation for Amazon sellers

- Use experiments when ad data indicates a listing conversion problem but the best creative/copy fix is uncertain.
- Test one major hypothesis at a time: main image CTR, title relevance, bullet objection handling, or A+ comparison proof.
- Avoid changing price, coupon, inventory, or ad mix dramatically during the test unless the experiment design accounts for it.

## Evidence required before action

- Eligibility in Seller Central, baseline sessions/orders/CVR, current content, hypothesis, traffic source stability.
- Ad search terms and review objections that justify the test variant.
- Experiment duration and confidence/result summary.

## Guardrails

- Do not treat experiment lift as universal if traffic mix changed materially.
- Do not run tests on unsupported claims or policy-sensitive content.
- Do not overreact to early results before the experiment has enough data.
