## What the official source establishes

- Bullet points communicate key product features, benefits, and use cases on the product detail page.
- Amazon maintains requirements and restrictions for bullet content; category-specific rules may apply.
- Bullets should be accurate, customer-facing, and consistent with the product being sold.
- Non-compliant or misleading bullet content can create listing quality or policy risk.

## Operational interpretation for Amazon sellers

- Use bullets to bridge ad search intent to concrete product proof: size, material, compatibility, use scenario, package contents, and differentiators.
- When a high-click search term is relevant but not converting, inspect whether bullets address the objection or attribute behind the query.
- Avoid dumping all keywords; prioritize buyer decision factors and claims that can be substantiated.

## Evidence required before action

- Top search terms, review objections, Q&A, competitor comparison, category attribute requirements.
- Existing bullets and product facts from manufacturer/spec sheet.
- Policy-sensitive claims such as medical, safety, warranty, compatibility, or performance claims.

## Guardrails

- Do not add unsupported claims or prohibited promotional language.
- Do not hide important compatibility or limitation information that drives returns.
- Do not use bullet edits as a substitute for fixing price, reviews, inventory, or offer problems.
