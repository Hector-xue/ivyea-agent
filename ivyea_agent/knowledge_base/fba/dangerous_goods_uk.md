# Amazon UK FBA dangerous-goods diagnostics

Source basis: authenticated UK Seller Central dangerous-goods identification and FBA review guidance.

## Required evidence

- UK marketplace, ASIN/FNSKU, product type, ingredients/materials, battery/aerosol/liquid attributes, packaging, and fulfilment route;
- exact dangerous-goods review state and requested SDS or exemption evidence;
- manufacturer, model, SDS revision/language, transport classification, battery chemistry/capacity, and labels;
- prior submissions and case responses.

## Guardrails

- Never infer non-hazardous status from marketing text alone.
- Do not alter an SDS or omit batteries/chemicals to bypass review.
- UK transport/product rules and Amazon FBA acceptance are separate checks.
