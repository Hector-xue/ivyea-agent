# Amazon Japan FBA dangerous-goods diagnostics

Source basis: authenticated Japan Seller Central dangerous-goods guidance and the live FBA review workflow.

## Required evidence

- JP marketplace, ASIN/FNSKU, product type, composition, battery/aerosol/liquid properties, packaging, and intended fulfilment;
- Japanese review message, requested SDS/exemption, document language/revision, and manufacturer;
- battery chemistry/capacity, transport classification, labels, and prior submissions;
- case replies and current inventory/listing state.

## Guardrails

- Do not import US/EU classification outcomes without Japan-specific confirmation.
- Preserve original Japanese requests and translate narrowly.
- Never conceal hazardous components or alter safety documents.
