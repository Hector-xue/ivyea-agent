# Amazon EU FBA dangerous-goods diagnostics

Source basis: authenticated European Seller Central dangerous-goods guidance and store-specific FBA review.

## Required evidence

- exact EU store/FC programme, ASIN/FNSKU, composition, physical form, battery/aerosol/liquid attributes, and packaging;
- review state, requested SDS/exemption, document language, revision date, and supplier/manufacturer;
- transport classification, labels, battery data, and store-specific compliance evidence;
- submission and case history.

## Guardrails

- An EU-market legal conclusion does not automatically prove FBA acceptance, and FBA acceptance does not prove legal compliance.
- Do not use one store's review as universal approval.
- Never modify safety data or product identity to evade classification.
