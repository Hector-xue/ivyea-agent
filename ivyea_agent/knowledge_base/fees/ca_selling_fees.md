# Amazon Canada selling fees and charge diagnostics

Source basis: Amazon Canada's current public pricing page. Fee values change; use the live page and account transaction records for the relevant date.

## Official model

- Costs can include the selling plan, category referral fees, fulfilment fees, optional programmes, storage, advertising, and other event-specific charges.
- Referral fees depend on category and the defined total sales price; FBA fees depend on the current size/weight and programme rules.
- Revenue Calculator and fee previews are estimates, while settlement/transaction records are observed charges.

## Required evidence

- CA marketplace, transaction date, fee type, SKU/ASIN, category, fulfilment programme, price, dimensions, weight, and tax context;
- the current Canadian fee schedule or calculator inputs;
- settlement/transaction line and any return, storage, removal, surcharge, or promotion.

## Guardrails

- Never apply US-currency, UK, or EU values to Canadian charges.
- Do not call an estimate a guaranteed invoice.
- GST/HST/PST/QST treatment requires current professional advice.
