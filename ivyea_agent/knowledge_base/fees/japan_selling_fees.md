# Amazon Japan selling fees and charge diagnostics

Source basis: Amazon Japan's public pricing page. The current Japanese page and account transaction records are authoritative.

## Official model

- Selling costs depend on plan, product category, fulfilment method, size/weight, price, optional programmes, and effective date.
- Referral, FBA, storage, refund-processing, and other charges use distinct rules.
- Revenue Calculator and fee previews are estimates; actual charges must be reconciled to transaction evidence.

## Required evidence

- JP marketplace, JPY currency, transaction date, fee type, category, SKU/ASIN, fulfilment route, dimensions, and weight;
- current Japanese pricing/FBA schedule and calculator inputs;
- settlement/transaction detail, returns, storage age, and programme participation.

## Guardrails

- Do not reuse US or European fee tables.
- Distinguish tax-inclusive and tax-exclusive displays.
- Consumption-tax conclusions require current professional advice.
