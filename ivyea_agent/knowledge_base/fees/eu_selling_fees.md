# Amazon EU selling fees and marketplace diagnostics

Source basis: Amazon Germany's public pricing page, with local European store schedules controlling each charge.

## Official model

- European selling fees vary by store, product category, selling plan, fulfilment route, size/weight, price band, and effective date.
- A unified European seller account does not create one universal fee table or tax treatment.
- Fee estimators are planning tools; the local settlement and transaction detail is the evidence for an actual charge.

## Required evidence

- exact store (DE/FR/IT/ES/NL/SE/PL/BE/IE), currency, transaction date, and programme;
- category, SKU/ASIN, price, dimensions/weight, fulfilment route, and inventory country;
- current local pricing page plus settlement/transaction evidence.

## Guardrails

- Do not use UK or Germany values as a blanket EU rate.
- Separate Amazon service fees from VAT, customs, EPR, and other legal obligations.
- Escalate cross-border VAT conclusions to a qualified adviser.
