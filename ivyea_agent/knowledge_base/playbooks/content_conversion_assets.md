# Content assets and conversion support

## Why advertising diagnosis must inspect content

A relevant ad click that does not convert is not always a traffic problem. Amazon
listing content, A+ modules, images, video, price, reviews, and offer quality can
all affect conversion. Suppressing relevant traffic before checking content can
hide a listing problem.

## Content levers

- Main image: clarity, product recognition, variant confusion, mobile readability.
- Secondary images: usage scenarios, size/spec proof, comparison, package contents.
- Bullet points: core benefit, compatibility, material/spec, use case, objection handling.
- A+ Content: enhanced images, text placements, comparison charts, brand story,
  and video where eligible.
- Brand Store / Brand Story: support branded traffic and repeat purchase paths.

## Advertising feedback signals

- High CTR + low CVR on relevant terms: listing/offer conversion issue is likely.
- Low CTR + relevant term: main image, title clarity, price, or placement may be weak.
- Winners missing from listing copy: add naturally to bullets, image text, A+, or Q&A;
  do not keyword-stuff the title.
- Competitor term clicks with no orders: check product differentiation and price gap
  before deciding whether to keep conquesting.

## Action boundary

Only mark a relevant term as a negative after checking whether content, price,
reviews, coupon, delivery promise, or variant mismatch explains the weak conversion.
