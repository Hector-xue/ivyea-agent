# Amazon UAE seller registration baseline

Source basis: Amazon UAE's public selling and registration guide. Current workflow requirements remain authoritative.

## Official baseline

- Amazon UAE lists business contact information, phone, national identity evidence, recent address/bank evidence, and a bank account for disbursements.
- The public guide describes Commercial Registration for an owner or authority evidence for a representative/account manager.
- Registration and identity verification are separate from later product, VAT, and category compliance.

## Diagnostic inputs

- UAE resident/non-resident context, entity type, Commercial Registration, and representative authority;
- Emirates ID or other exact identity request shown by Amazon;
- recent statement/utility evidence, bank-account holder, and submitted address;
- exact rejection, step, timestamp, and requested remediation.

## Guardrails

- Do not promise that registration proves VAT, customs, or product compliance.
- Do not provide definitive UAE licensing or VAT advice without current professional review.
- Do not alter truthful identity, authority, or company records to force a match.
