# Amazon UK and Europe seller registration baseline

Source basis: Amazon UK seller registration guide. This card applies to the UK/European unified registration described by the cited guide; it must not be used as a substitute for the requirements displayed in a seller's live registration workflow.

## Official flow

The public UK guide separates registration into six stages: business information, seller/primary-contact information, billing information, store and product information, bank account information, and identity verification/document collection.

The guide states that European unified registration can create access to the UK, Germany, Italy, Spain, France, Netherlands, Sweden, and Poland marketplaces. Access does not prove that a seller has completed tax, product-compliance, fulfillment, or local legal obligations in every store.

## Evidence required for diagnosis

- registration marketplace/region and the exact step that is blocked;
- legal entity type, country of establishment, and the exact company name/address submitted;
- the document type requested by the live workflow and the issuing authority;
- bank-account holder and business/primary-contact relationship;
- exact rejection banner/email, timestamp, and case reference;
- whether the problem occurs in identity verification, business verification, payment verification, or a later country-specific compliance review.

## Safe checks

- Compare legal name, registration number, registered address, directors/current status, and primary-contact authority with the official business document.
- Use the document requirements shown for the seller's entity and marketplace; do not infer that the US document list is sufficient.
- Treat EU/UK tax questions as jurisdiction- and inventory-flow-specific. Do not give a definitive VAT conclusion without the seller's establishment, inventory storage, dispatch/destination countries, and current professional advice.

## Guardrails

- Do not promise approval or a fixed verification time.
- Do not advise changing truthful legal data merely to make documents match.
- Do not upload edited/fabricated documents or bypass the verification route.
- Do not treat linked marketplace access as proof that every marketplace is ready for sale.
