# Amazon Singapore seller registration baseline

Source basis: Amazon Singapore's public step-by-step registration page. Requirements may change and must be rechecked in the live workflow.

## Official baseline

- Amazon Singapore publishes a marketplace-specific registration flow and business-verification requirements.
- The public page distinguishes business verification from seller identity verification and asks for evidence linking the primary contact to the business.
- The exact accepted certificate, identity document, language, and validity rules are controlled by the current registration page.

## Diagnostic inputs

- Singapore or overseas entity type, legal name, registration number, and status;
- business-verification certificate and beneficial-owner/legal-representative relationship;
- primary-contact identity document and exact rejection text;
- bank, billing, phone, and address consistency.

## Guardrails

- Do not infer Singapore requirements from US or EU registration.
- Do not store raw identity documents in the knowledge base.
- Never advise fabricated association evidence or edited government records.
