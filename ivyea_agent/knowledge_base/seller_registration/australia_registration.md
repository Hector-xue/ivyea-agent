# Amazon Australia seller registration baseline

Source basis: Amazon Australia's public registration guide. The seller's live workflow is authoritative for requested evidence.

## Official baseline

- Registration covers business, seller, billing, store/product, and identity-verification information.
- Individual and business sellers can receive different document requests.
- Business verification can require government ID, a business-verification document, proof of authority, and recent proof of address.

## Diagnostic inputs

- entity type and country, ABN/ACN or other registration evidence when applicable;
- primary contact and authority to represent the entity;
- exact identity/address document request and rejection message;
- existing linked selling accounts and any live account-health prerequisite shown by Amazon.

## Guardrails

- Do not assume an ABN/ACN or a specific document is required for every entity type.
- Do not bypass linked-account or account-health checks.
- Use current Australian tax/legal advice for GST and entity questions.
